Current Scenario

On average, an Indian household discards a significant amount of usable clothing each year, contributing to growing textile waste and environmental strain. At the same time, countless individuals across the country lack access to basic clothing, highlighting a stark imbalance in resource distribution. The lack of awareness, fast fashion habits, and limited donation channels further worsen the problem. To tackle clothing waste, India needs stronger initiatives, public awareness campaigns, and better infrastructure for collection and redistribution. By embracing sustainable clothing practices and donating unused garments, we can significantly reduce waste and bring dignity to those in need.
